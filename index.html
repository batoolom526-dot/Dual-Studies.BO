About Dual Studies

Since 2015, Dual Study programs based on the German model have been offered at Al-Quds University. Supported by the project "More Job Opportunities for Palestinian Youth II" (GIZ, on behalf of BMZ), these B.A. programs in economics, engineering, and IT combine theory and practice, producing graduates who are well-prepared for the job market and valuable to local companies.

International Network

International experience is key for both students and the Palestinian economy. To strengthen knowledge transfer, a network is being built to support three-month internships in Germany. Join this network and benefit from motivated, multilingual interns.
